Wings of Fire

by Arun Tiwari

Wings of Fire is an autobiographical account of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, written in collaboration with Arun Tiwari. This masterpiece offers an inspiring narrative of India’s "Missile Man," encapsulating his journey from a humble beginning in the small town of Rameswaram to becoming one of India’s most celebrated scientists and the 11th President of India. The book is a testimony to the power of dreams, perseverance, and the role of education in personal and national development.

The 3 mistakes of my life

by Chetan Bhagat

Chetan Bhagat’s The 3 Mistakes of My Life is a compelling novel that explores the lives, ambitions, and challenges of three young men in Ahmedabad, India. The story, set in the early 2000s, weaves together themes of friendship, ambition, love, and the socio-political undercurrents of the time. The book is narrated through the perspective of Govind Patel, a budding entrepreneur, and is inspired by real events and contemporary issues.

Harry Potter

by J.K.Rowling

The Harry Potter series by J.K. Rowling is a globally celebrated fantasy saga that captures the journey of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his battle against the dark forces led by Lord Voldemort. Spanning seven books, the series is a masterful blend of adventure, friendship, love, and the eternal fight between good and evil. It has become one of the most beloved and influential works of contemporary literature.
